Basketball Lineup Optimizer

R-based statistical analysis for optimizing team lineups through player synergy modeling

This project uses R to analyze player statistics and pairwise synergy metrics, then applies linear optimization to recommend the best possible 5-man lineups. It processes how pairs of players perform together on the court.

Built from a passion for basketball and data science, the optimizer turns lineup decisions from gut instinct into math.

Built with
