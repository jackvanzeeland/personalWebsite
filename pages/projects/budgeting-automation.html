Budgeting Automation

UiPath bot that processes bank statements and categorizes transactions with 95% accuracy

Built out of frustration with manual budgeting, this UiPath RPA bot reads bank statement CSVs and PDFs, classifies each transaction into budget categories, and outputs a formatted Excel report. It handles multiple bank formats and adapts to new merchants over time.

The bot cut monthly budgeting from hours of tedious data entry down to about 5 minutes of review. It processes statements from 3+ different banks and maintains 95% categorization accuracy across thousands of transactions.
