Super Bowl Competition

Project Overview

This project involved creating a system for a Super Bowl prop bet competition. Participants ranked their confidence in 20 prop outcomes, and the tool validated their confidence values, compared predictions to real results, and calculated each participant's score to determine the winner.

How It Works

The system automates the scoring and validation of Super Bowl prop bets, ensuring fair and accurate results.

                                D --> E[Determine Winner];
Technologies Used
  • Python: For data processing, validation, and score calculation.
  • Creative Problem Solving: To design the competition rules and scoring logic.
